Our story

Most AI projects fail.
We know why.

Before Valae, our founding team spent years building AI inside major international brands. We kept seeing the same pattern. Companies ran bloated, expensive AI projects that took months to deliver and never delivered measurable results.

The problem was always the same. Solutions assumed well-organised data, which no real company has. According to MIT, 95% of AI projects fail despite 90% of employees using AI tools. Because knowledge is scattered. Business information just isn't available to AI.

We founded Valae on the belief that there's a better way. Step Zero was born from this insight: before you build any AI tool, you need to organise the knowledge it draws from. That foundation — the AI brain — is what makes every tool that follows actually work.
