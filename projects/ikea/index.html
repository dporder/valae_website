Overview

Making the best better

IKEA’s Billie has managed 3.2M+ multilingual interactions to date, and has led to the reskilling of 8,500 call center workers into interior design advisors.

Valae is now embedded in IKEA's global team, applying systematic technical improvements to Billie’s framework to maximize business impact and ROI.

Scaling the experience to 63 countries

Billie handles millions of conversations already. Now Valae is helping scale that across the world.

The Challenge

Scale creates its own problems

Billie is one of retail's most high-profile AI deployments. But at IKEA's scale, complexity compounds fast. Every improvement must work across dozens of markets, languages, and product categories simultaneously.

Prompt complexity at scale
Billie’s multi-layered decision engine grew too complex to update without a systematic, global restructuring.
Disconnected quality tools
Without connected feedback loops, improvements were often driven by intuition rather than empirical data.
Cascading failures in understanding
Minor misinterpretations of customer needs led to incorrect answers and unnecessary human escalations.
Support leaving money on the table
High-intent buying signals were often treated as routine support tasks, missing critical opportunities to convert.
Knowledge at scale
Manually managing documentation for 63 markets is unsustainable, allowing outdated product or policy information to persist.
Multi-market complexity
Across 63 countries, optimizing AI performance for one region’s circumstances risks breaking logic in another.
The Solution

Six workstreams. One smarter chatbot

Valae is applying the Step Zero methodology across six workstreams, each designed to make Billie measurably better.

System architecture
Restructuring the entire prompt system into modular blocks, so they can be rearranged like LEGO bricks.
Robust evaluation
Building the feedback loop that surfaces errors, prioritises fixes, and validates changes before they reach customers.
Conversational commerce
Detecting buying intent, building in sales steering, and measuring chatbot-driven revenue.
Voice AI
Contributing to Billie's expansion into voice-based customer service, a new channel requiring different strategies.
Knowledge management
Planning automated pipelines that detect flag document changes, generate updates, and check for terminology drift.
Intent classification
Testing small, fast language models to improve the understanding of customer needs.
