Overview

Making the best better

IKEA's Billie is retail's most ambitious AI chatbot programme — a generative AI-powered assistant that has handled over 3.2 million customer interactions across 63 markets and dozens of languages.

Valae was brought in to make Billie smarter. Where most AI projects start from scratch, this one starts from excellence — and the challenge is making it measurably, systematically better.

Serving customers across 63 countries

Millions of conversations across dozens of markets and languages, around the clock.

The Challenge

Scale creates its own problems

Billie is one of retail's most high-profile AI deployments. But at IKEA's scale, complexity compounds fast. Every improvement must work across dozens of markets, languages, and product categories simultaneously.

Prompt complexity at scale
Billie's behaviour is driven by layered prompt systems that grow organically and become increasingly hard to improve at global scale.
Disconnected quality tools
Scoring systems, validation tools, and monitoring dashboards existed but weren't connected into a continuous feedback loop.
Classification leaking value
Misclassifying customer intent cascades into wrong answers, unnecessary escalations, and contaminated quality data.
Leaving money on the table
Customers signal buying intent in support conversations. The chatbot treated these as support queries and moved on.
Knowledge at scale
Content spanning 63 markets and dozens of languages, with no way to detect when information goes quietly stale.
Multi-market complexity
63 markets with different languages, regulations, and expectations. Changes in one market risk degrading another.
The Solution

Six workstreams. One smarter chatbot.

Valae is embedded within IKEA's global GenAI chatbot team, applying the same Step Zero methodology at a fundamentally different scale.

Prompt Architecture
Restructuring the entire prompt system into modular, intent-specific prompts with continuous improvement cycles.
Quality Evaluation
Building the feedback loop: monitor, detect failure modes, prioritise fixes, evaluate impact, deploy.
Conversational Commerce
Detecting buying intent in support conversations and routing toward conversion.
Voice AI
Contributing to Billie's expansion into voice-based customer service with new prompting strategies.
Knowledge Architecture
Automated pipelines for staleness detection, source change monitoring, and terminology consistency across 63 markets.
Intent Classification
Small, fast language models achieving 80%+ accuracy at sub-400ms latency in early experiments.
From Support to Sales

Turning every conversation into an opportunity

Customers signal buying intent inside support conversations. Valae is building a support-to-sales bridge that detects purchase signals, routes toward conversion, and measures chatbot-driven revenue — transforming Billie from a cost-reduction tool into a revenue engine.
