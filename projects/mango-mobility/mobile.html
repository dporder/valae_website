Overview

The leading mobility brand in the Netherlands

Mango Mobility sells 500+ products, from mobility scooters and electric bikes to wheelchairs and small automobiles.

But behind that scale, there was no technical team and knowledge was scattered across files, chats, inboxes, and legacy tools.

The Challenge

Drowning in repetitive questions

Staff across 14 locations were fielding the same questions. Product specs, pricing, availability. Even simple tasks ate up time.

The problem wasn't a lack of AI tools. It was a lack of foundation. Without organised knowledge, no AI tool can give accurate answers.

The Solution

One AI brain. Multiple automations.

We ran Step Zero, ingesting Mango's scattered data into a single AI brain. Then we deployed two ROI-focused AI tools on top of it.

1
Ingest
Pulled in data from Excel files, email threads, their website, calls with staff, legacy systems like Vendit, and more.
2
Structure
De-duplicated, cleaned, and tagged every piece of knowledge into a unified format, the AI brain.
3
Deploy
Built two AI tools on top of the brain, one to provide instant info to staff, and another to talk with customers by email automatically.
Tool 1

Knowledge Assistant

Gives staff instant, source-cited answers to any internal question, from product specs to pricing to procedures to advice and guidance.
